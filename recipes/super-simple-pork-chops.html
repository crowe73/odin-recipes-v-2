Super Simple Pork Chops

Description

Pork chops with a sweet glaze.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless pork loin chops
  • 1/4 cup of apple juice
  • 1/4 cup of maple syrup

Steps

  1. Heat large skillet over medium-high heat. Brown pork chops in pan for about 4 to 5 minutes per side. Pour in apple juice and maple syrup; reduce heat and simmer unil the juice has reduced and thickened slightly.
  2. Cook until pork is no longer pink in the center. Internal temperature should be 145 degrees F.
