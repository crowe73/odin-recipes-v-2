Homemade Tacos

Description

Homemade tacos are the perfect busy weeknight dinner. These easy ground beef tacos are better than take-out and ready in minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. 90% to 93% lean ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon of chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 3/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • !/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup of tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 12 taco shells - either hard shells or small 6 inch soft flour tortillas will work
  • Optional taco toppings: shredded cheese, shredded lettuce, chopped tomatoes, diced onions, taco sauce, sour cream, guacamole, etc.

Steps

  1. Add ground beef to a large skillet over medium-high heat. Break the meat apart with a wooden spoon. Add chili powder, cumin, salt, oregano, garlic powder, and pepper to the meat. Stir well. Cook until the meat is cooked through, about 6 to 8 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  2. Reduce heat to medium. Add the tomato sauce and water. Stir to combine. cook, sitrring occasionally, for 7 to 8 minutes, until some of the liquid evaporates but the meat mixture is still a little saucy. Remove from the heat.
  3. Warm taco shell according to the package directions.
  4. Fill the taco shells with 2 heaping tablespoons of taco meat. Top with desired taco toppings: shredded cheese, shredded lettuce, chopped tomatoes, diced onions, taco sauce, sour cream, guacamole, etc.
